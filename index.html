Adventures in Fire Island

My recent escape to Fire Island was exactly the breath of fresh air I needed. Free from the noise of cars, the island instantly relaxes you with its endless stretches of pristine white sand beaches and the soothing sound of the Atlantic surf. Walking through the sunken forest and exploring the historic lighthouse were absolute highlights of the trip.

Whether you are looking to hike through unique maritime forests, catch a stunning sunset over the Great South Bay, or just layout with a good book, this coastal paradise has a strange way of making time slow down. It’s easily one of New York's best-kept natural treasures.
